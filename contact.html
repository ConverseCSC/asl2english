If you have a question about this project, or you would like to report an error with the site please contact:

    Gretchen Marlow at gkmarlow001@converse.edu

An error with the site can include any instances where you believe a sign may have been improperly translated or had its parameters incorrectly categorized. We want the site to be as accurate as possible. You are also encouraged to report any problems you have with the site itself.

If you believe a sign that should be available is missing, please first check the signdata.js file on our GitHub repository for the sign. If the sign is not there, then please send your suggestion to the above email.

If you are looking to use this site in your own research, we would love to hear about your work! You may contact us at the above email. Check out our About page for more information on licensing and permissions.

Thank you for making the site better!
