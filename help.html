General Questions

What is ASL?
ASL stands for American Sign Language. ASL is a sign language used by many deaf and hard-of-hearing communities in North America. Click here for more information on American Sign Language.

What is an ASL-to-English Dictionary?
An ASL-to-English dictionary allows you to search for an ASL sign through means other than just its English translation. You are able to take the visible characteristics of a sign, and search with those to find the meaning.

Choosing Parameters

When searching, there are five parameters to set: number of hands, handshape, palm orientation, location, and movement. You do not need to set all the parameters, but the results will be more accurate if you do so. The most important parameters are number of hands and handshape.
